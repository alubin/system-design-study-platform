## Recommended Study Plan

### Week 1-2: Build Foundation
- **Daily**: 30 min topics + 15 min flashcards
- Cover all 6 categories at high level
- Focus on understanding, not memorization

### Week 3-4: Deep Dive
- **Daily**: 1 topic deep-dive + 20 min flashcards
- Start practice problems (1-2 per week)
- Take category quizzes

### Week 5+: Interview Prep
- **Daily**: 30 min practice problems + flashcard review
- Take full quizzes (all categories)
- Review weak areas from quiz results

## Keyboard Shortcuts

**Flashcards:**
- `Space` - Flip card
- `1` - Don't know (review soon)
- `2` - Hard (review in 1 day)
- `3` - Good (standard interval)
- `4` - Easy (longer interval)

## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency > Intensity**
   - 30 min/day > 3 hours/weekend

2. **Use Spaced Repetition**
   - Review flashcards daily
   - Don't skip overdue cards

3. **Practice Out Loud**
   - Explain concepts as if teaching
   - Use the ELI5 explanations as a guide

4. **Track Your Progress**
   - Export data weekly (Settings â†’ Export)
   - Review quiz history for weak areas

5. **Before the Interview**
   - Take all quizzes 2 days before
   - Review all practice problems
   - Skim through common mistakes

## Study Modes Explained

| Mode | Best For | Time Needed |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Topics** | Learning new concepts | 15-30 min |
| **Flashcards** | Daily review & retention | 10-15 min |
| **Practice** | Interview simulation | 45-90 min |
| **Quiz** | Self-assessment | 10-20 min |

## Data Management

- **Stored locally**: In your browser (localStorage)
- **Backup**: Settings â†’ Export Progress (recommended weekly)
- **Switch devices**: Export from Device A, Import on Device B

## Need Help?
